01 — About

Strategy, consulting & execution
at the intersection of ideas and impact

I'm a BCom (Hons) student at SRCC, University of Delhi (Class of 2028) with a strong orientation toward consulting, strategy, and high-stakes problem-solving. I combine sharp analytical thinking with the ability to work across institutions, stakeholders, and high-pressure environments — academic rigor is my baseline, not my ceiling.

As a 4× Case Finalist on national and international panels — including MBB-judged rounds and a global competition against teams from the University of Bath and Newcastle — I've built a consulting mindset through real, competitive work. At Business Conclave SRCC, Asia's largest undergrad management fest, I drove institutional outreach engaging the Delhi CM's Office and RBI Governor's Office, translating strategic priorities into stakeholder outcomes.

On the BD and entrepreneurial side, I founded VaultX, growing it to 200+ mentored aspirants through product development, go-to-market strategy, and peer-driven community building. Finance rounds out my toolkit — from credit analysis and deal scouting to equity research — and I'm actively pursuing the CFA Level 1 qualification to deepen that foundation.
